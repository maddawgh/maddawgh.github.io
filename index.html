The Realization

My journey didn't start in an engineering lab. It started in a hospital waiting room.

I was fascinated by the sheer scale of the machines around me. When I saw a CT Scanner in operation, I realized that modern medicine is as much about mechanical precision as it is about biology.

That moment defined my career: I didn't just want to build machines; I wanted to build machines that save lives.
